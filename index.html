The Concept

It is with the utmost reverence and anticipation that the C. Parma Restaurant Group announces the grand opening of our flagship establishment at 8801 Wilson Boulevard, Arlington, Virginia 22201. We stand poised to introduce to the discerning palates of the District metropolitan area a culinary concept unprecedented in its singular dedication: the complete and total elevation of Chicken Parmesan to its rightful place among the world's most revered gastronomic masterpieces.

For centuries, Pollo alla Parmigiana has occupied a sacred position within the canon of Italian culinary tradition. More than mere sustenance, this preparation represents the very essence of convivialità—the profound Italian philosophy that recognizes the dinner table as the center of human connection. At C. Parma, we approach this heritage with the gravity it demands, treating each cutlet not as protein, but as a canvas upon which we paint edible narratives of cultural exchange.

Under the visionary guidance of Executive Chef Salvatore Palmieri, our kitchen has undertaken a lifelong odyssey to transport this iconic preparation across continents and through centuries of culinary evolution. Chef Palmieri's extensive travels through Asia, Europe, the Caribbean, and the Americas have yielded a compendium of preparations that honor local terroir while maintaining an unwavering commitment to the foundational excellence of the Parmigiano-Reggiano crust.

This dedication to craft has garnered recognition from the James Beard Foundation, the Michelin Guide, and the World's 50 Best Restaurants organization, each acknowledging Chef Palmieri's audacious reimagining of a classic while preserving its soul. We invite you to experience the culmination of this gastronomic research—a menu that traverses the globe while remaining firmly rooted in Italian tradition.
